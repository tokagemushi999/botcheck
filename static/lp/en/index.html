Analysis Engines

6 perspectives, one verdict

Timing Analysis
Detects bot-like patterns from posting intervals, response speed, and activity hours
Style Analysis
Identifies AI-generated text from message length, vocabulary diversity, and punctuation habits
Behavior Analysis
Evaluates human-likeness from channel usage patterns, reply rates, and reaction tendencies
AI Detection
Identifies AI-specific traces like templated patterns, unnatural politeness, and excessive consistency
Profile Analysis
Detects bot-specific traits from account age, avatar, and username patterns
Network Analysis
Evaluates social human-likeness from conversation reciprocity, mention relationships, and isolation
